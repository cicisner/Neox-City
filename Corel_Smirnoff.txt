File: Corel Smirnoff

Name: Corel Smirnoff
Age: 25 years
Race: Human
Gender: M
Height: 5 ft 9 in /  180 cm
Weight: 162lb /  73kg

Corel Smirnoff was born in Neox City to Linus and Debian Smirnoff. The eldest of the Smirnoff children, Corel managed to slip under the radar for quite some time, so not much is known about him. What is known, however, is that he is one of the most innocent appearing of the Smirnoffs as his abilities aren't quite as blatantly adversarial of that of his siblings, but he is quite proficient with the written word and is quite charming to boot. There has been some tell of his conniving ability to convince a person to let him in anywhere and under any conditions. These stories are as of yet unconfirmed, but he is a threat nonetheless and will be kept under surveillance.
