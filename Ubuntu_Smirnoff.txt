File: Ubuntu Smirnoff

Name: Ubuntu Smirnoff
Age: 23
Gender: M
Height: 5 ft 5 in /  165cm
Weight: 130lb /  62kg

Ubuntu has shown great skill in artificing from a young age. Born in Neox City, it was unsurprising that he was exposed to constructs and the like practically from birth, but few children show such interest in the matter, and fewer still show as much talent at it. His skill is certainly a wonder. He does own his own shop of sorts, selling parts and offering "technological support" and other such nonsensical pandering to the common people. It is hoped that he will continue to use his talent in such a useless way if he is not going to use it for the betterment of the city. He will be kept under close surveillance as he could quickly become a threat.
